# Acme Home Services FAQ

Demo content for testing the chatbot. Replace with your own docs.

## Booking

Q: How do I book a cleaning?
A: Through the website booking page or by messaging us on WhatsApp. Same-day slots close at 10am.

Q: Can I reschedule?
A: Yes, free up to 12 hours before the appointment. Inside 12 hours we charge 50% of the visit.

Q: Which areas do you cover?
A: Downtown, Marina district and the North suburbs. We don't cover the airport zone yet.

## Pricing

Q: What does a standard cleaning cost?
A: Studio 90, one bedroom 120, two bedroom 160, three bedroom 210. Deep cleaning is roughly 1.6x standard.

Q: Do you charge extra for supplies?
A: No, supplies and equipment are included. If you want us to use your own products, tell us in the booking notes.

Q: Is there a cancellation fee?
A: Free if you cancel more than 12 hours ahead. After that, 50%. No-shows are billed in full.

## Payments

Q: How do I pay?
A: Card on file after each visit, or monthly invoice for recurring clients. We don't take cash.

Q: When am I charged?
A: The evening after the visit, once you've had a chance to review the work.

## Service

Q: How long does a visit take?
A: Standard cleaning is 2 to 3 hours for most apartments. Deep cleaning runs 4 to 6.

Q: Do I need to be home?
A: No. Most clients leave a key code or a lockbox. Teams are background checked and insured.

Q: What if something gets damaged?
A: Report it within 48 hours and our insurance covers repair or replacement up to 2,000 per incident.
